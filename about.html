About TM Joinery Ltd

Experienced joiners based in Eaglesham, serving Glasgow and beyond

Professional Joinery Services in Glasgow

We are an expanding business, taking pride in transforming homes and delivering on client expectations with every project.

We offer a wide range of joinery services including extensions, refurbishments, conversions, and kitchen installations to transform your home. We are passionate about creating spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful. Whether you're dreaming of a bespoke deck to elevate your outdoor living or custom cabinetry to enhance your interior, we're here to bring your vision to life.

At TM Joinery Ltd, attention to detail and customer satisfaction are at the heart of what we do. From concept to completion, we work closely with you to ensure every detail is delivered to perfection.

Meet Euan & Kieran

Euan and Kieran have worked together for a number of years prior to forming TM Joinery Ltd, combining their shared experience to successfully complete various projects ranging from extensions, kitchens, refurbishments to bespoke decking.

They have a passion for their craft and work closely with the client to ensure that each detail aligns with their vision. They are adept in project management skills to ensure each phase of a project runs smoothly, on schedule and meets the highest standards of quality.
